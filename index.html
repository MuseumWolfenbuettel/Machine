9.Machine – Maschine

Whether it was the "Brandes iron foundry", "Ravensberg spinning mill" or the "Welger Brothers‘" machine factory, Wolfenbüttel's industrial age companies settled along the new railway line. In 1861, gas lighting illuminated the streets of the town centre for the first time, and the supply of electric power in the early 20th century finally turned night to day. The brightly-lit factory buildings, in which people could now work day and night, represented not only technical progress, but also the shady side of light.
